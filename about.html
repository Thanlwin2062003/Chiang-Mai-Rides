Our Story

Providing quality rentals and unforgettable experiences since 2010

Who We Are

Chiang Mai Rides was founded by a group of travel enthusiasts who wanted to make exploring Northern Thailand easier and more enjoyable for visitors. What started as a small motorbike rental shop has grown into Chiang Mai's premier rental service for both bikes and cars.

Our team consists of locals and expats who are passionate about sharing the beauty of Northern Thailand. We don't just rent vehicles - we provide personalized recommendations, route planning, and 24/7 support to ensure you have the best possible experience.

Our Mission

To provide safe, reliable transportation options that allow travelers to discover Chiang Mai and its surroundings at their own pace, with the confidence of local expertise behind them.
