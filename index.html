Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to rent a vehicle?

You'll need a valid passport and an international driver's permit (IDP) along with your home country driver's license. For Thai nationals, just your Thai driver's license is sufficient.

Do you offer one-way rentals?

Yes, we offer one-way rentals to several destinations in Northern Thailand including Pai, Chiang Rai, and Mae Hong Son. Additional fees may apply depending on the distance.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made more than 48 hours before pickup receive a full refund. Within 48 hours, a 50% cancellation fee applies. No-shows are charged the full rental amount.

Is insurance included in the rental price?

All rentals include basic insurance with a deductible. You can upgrade to zero-deductible insurance for an additional daily fee when you pick up your vehicle.
