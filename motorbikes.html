Riding Safely in Chiang Mai

Always Wear a Helmet

Thai law requires helmets, and we provide high-quality helmets with all rentals.

Traffic Rules

Drive on the left side of the road and be cautious at intersections.

Weather Awareness

Rainy season (June-Oct) can make roads slippery - adjust your speed.

Navigation

We provide phone mounts and can help set up GPS navigation.

Not comfortable riding alone? Join one of our guided tours with experienced local guides.
