Tour Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need riding experience to join a tour?

For our scooter tours, basic riding experience is sufficient. For motorbike and adventure tours, we recommend at least 1 year of riding experience. We assess all riders before departure for safety.

What should I bring on the tour?

Comfortable clothing, closed-toe shoes, sunglasses, sunscreen, and your camera. For multi-day tours, we'll provide a packing list. We carry basic first aid and repair kits.

What happens if it rains?

We provide rain gear and adjust routes if needed for safety. Tours proceed in light rain. For heavy rain, we may reschedule or offer alternative activities.

Can non-riders join the tours?

Yes! We offer options for non-riders to join in support vehicles or as passengers on some tours. Contact us for arrangements.

Have more questions? Contact our tour specialists who can help plan your perfect adventure.
